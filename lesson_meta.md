## 教學目標 (Teaching Objectives)

- 中文：
  本課程是針對精算科學、風險管理與保險或相關領域專業的大學生常用之應用科學資料分析方法的應用介紹課程，屬於產險資料分析之授課與應用技能課程。每堂課前鼓勵學生先閱讀指定教材，並透過課堂參與、作業與報告展現學習成果。課程目的在於說明教材中有趣或困難之內容、進行技能訓練與演練，並提供討論場域。課程目標如下：
  1. 學習產險相關之基礎資料分析原理。
  2. 學習如何進行產險相關資料分析。
  3. 學習產險資料分析相關統計軟體。
- English:
  This course is an applied introduction to data analysis methods commonly used in applied sciences for undergraduate students majoring in Actuarial Science, Risk Management & Insurance, or related field. This is a lecture and applied skills course on property and casualty insurance-related data analysis. Before each class, students are encouraged to read assigned materials. Students are expected to demonstrate their learning via classroom participation, assignments, and reports. The class lectures elaborate on interesting or difficult materials, conduct skill-building exercises and demonstrations, and provide a forum for discussion. The goals are:
  1. Learn the principles of basic data analysis related to property and casualty insurance.
  2. Learn how to conduct data analysis related to property and casualty insurance.
  3. Learn relevant statistical software for property and casualty insurance-related data analysis.

## 教學綱要 (Course Description)

- 中文：
  本課程主要包含下列事項：
  1. 學習如何處理資料、以圖形及表格呈現資料，並應用合適統計方法分析資料。
  2. 透過實際產險資料分析，將理論與方法實際應用，引導學生思考與分析，培養解決實務問題能力，以及產險資料分析與解釋能力。
  3. 培養基本統計分析能力。
- English:
  This course mainly covers the following subjects:
  1. How to process data, present data in graphs and tables, and apply appropriate statistical methods.
  2. Through analyzing actual property and casualty insurance-related data, students apply learned theories and methods, and develop practical analysis and problem-solving ability.
  3. Training in basic statistical analysis.
